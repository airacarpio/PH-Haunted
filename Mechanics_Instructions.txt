Mechanics:
You are locked inside the Laperal house at the middle of the night, your main goal is to escape while being attacked by the ghosts. Get all the keys asked to use as an escape from the house. Each level requires a particular number of keys to finish the level. The player must tap the screen to enables the character to jump over the ghost. He/she must not touch the ghost to avoid losing life or keys. Once he touched the ghost, 1 key from the keys he already collected will be deducted. 
Instructions:
1.	To avoid the ghosts, jump over them by tapping the screen.
2.	Collect all the keys, to escape the haunted house
3.	Do not let yourself be touched by the ghost to avoid losing your life or keys.
